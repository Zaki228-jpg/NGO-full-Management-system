Who We Are

REHA Organization is a humanitarian and development organization working to support vulnerable communities across Afghanistan. Our programs focus on education, health services, emergency assistance, and women empowerment. We are committed to improving the quality of life for people through sustainable development and community support.

Our Core Values

Transparency

We ensure openness and honesty in all our activities and programs.

Accountability

We are responsible to the communities we serve and our partners.

Humanity

We believe in protecting human dignity and supporting vulnerable people.

Respect

We respect cultural diversity and the rights of all communities.

Community Commitment

We work closely with communities to create sustainable development.
